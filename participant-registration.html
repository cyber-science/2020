Participant Registration (CyberSciTech/PICom/DASC/CBDCom and all associated Special Sessions & Workshop)

  1. Each paper will require one ADVANCE REGISTRATION by 20 June 2019. Please note that if your full payment is not received by that date, your paper will be removed from the proceedings. This deadline will be strictly enforced.

  2. For students, paper co-authors (with one advance registration already), and general participations, only a BASIC REGISTRATION is required. The basic registration can be done between 25 May 2019 - 26 July 2019.

  3. When registering, your participant code (PCode) is your conference acronym (CyberSciTech, DASC, PICom, CBDCom, BDVC, CSC&CEA, CyberIoT, HISSD, EDCSTA) followed by your paper ID (the 10 digit ID number provided by EDAS).

  4. For non-author participants, please use the acronym SPA and write 787 as your Paper ID.

  5. Detailed registration instructions are provided at the online registration page: https://www.nishitetsutravel.jp/niccs/ieee4/

  6. Please note that for each registered paper, at least one of the authors must present their work at the conference, otherwise their papers will be removed from the IEEE digital library after the conference.
