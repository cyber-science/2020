Conference Venues and Local Information

General Information:

Welcome to Fukuoka City, the city venue of the IEEE 2019 CyberSciTech/DASC/PICom/CBDCom Co-located Conferences and Associated Events.

Fukuoka City is in the island of Kyushu, on the southwest part of Japan. Fukuoka City has mainly two areas, that is, Tenjin which is more or less the CBD (Central Business District) area, and Hakata where the Hakata Station is located and serving the Shinkansen (Bullet trains) and JR (Japan Railway) lines. There are lots of department stores, duty-free shops, choices of hotels (at your budget), and restaurants in any of the areas. There are buses (¥100 one-way) that goes around in a loop route (also stops in designated bus stops) between Hakata station and Tenjin. You can also take other local Nishitetsu buses passing along the route, at the same bus fare, ¥100.

Air, Land, and Sea Access to Fukuoka:

International/Domestic Flights, Shinkansen (Bullet) Trains, and Access to City Center from Fukuoka Airport https://www.welcome-fukuoka.or.jp/english/395.html. From Busan (Korea), there’s a Jetfoil service (2hrs 55min) or Ferry (about 6 hrs) to Hakata International Port Terminal. Fukuoka City provides a good transportation connections to other parts of Kyushu Island. (Source: Fukuoka Convention and Visitors Bureau)

From Fukuoka airport, most hotels and major points downtown can be reached by subway, bus, or taxi within 10-20 minutes.

Hotel Accommodations:

There are many hotels (at your choice and budget) surrounding Hakata Station and its vicinity which are just a few minutes -walk from Hakata Station. Others hotels are spread out in Tenjin, or within walking distance from subway stations or bus stops in the city.

For conference participants/attendees, and for your convenience, it is suggested that you stay at hotels near Hakata Station. Please book your hotel accommodations to a booking site of your choice.

Conference venues:

The conference will be held at Hotel Nikko Fukuoka, located near Hakata Station (about 3-min. walk). The second day of the conference and Welcome Reception will be at Kyushu Sangyo University.

Access to Kyushu Sangyo University:

By train: Take the local train (Futsuu densha) bound for either Kokura/Moji/Mojiko from Hakata Station to KyuSanDai Mae Station which is along the main JR Kagoshima line. Train departs every 10-15 minutes (depending on time of day). The local train ride is about 15-min and the fare is ¥280.

You can also take the rapid train (Kaisoku densha), but since this train doesn’t stop at KyuSanDai Mae station, you have to get-off at Kashii Station (the station before), wait and transfer to the next local train; or get-off at FuKouDai Mae station (next to KyuSanDai Mae), wait and transfer to the local train (back track) bound for Hakata, and disembark at the next station (KyuSanDai Mae). To avoid the hassle of waiting, transferring trains, and to save time, just take the local train from Hakata Station.

By bus: If you will be staying in Tenjin area. You can take the Nishitetsu Highway Bus (Rosen Bus) from Tenjin YubinKyoku Mae (in front of Tenjin Post Office) and get-off at KyuSanDai Minami-guchi (Kyushu Sangyo Univ. South Gate) bus stop. It’s about 5-min walk to the conference venue. The bus ride from Tenjin YuubinKyoku Mae to KyuSanDai Minami-guchi is about 15~20 min. (depending on time of day), and cost ¥430.

Note: There is no direct bus route service from Hakata station to KyuSanDai Mae Minami-guchi bus stop.
