Call for Papers

IEEE PICOM 2020 CALLS FOR:

  • Regular conference paper: 6 - 8 pages, submitted to one of four tracks
  • WiP/workshop/special session paper: 4-6 pages, and poster paper: 2-4 pages
  • Late breaking innovation paper: We seek the latest research and development work. The submission can be of any type, and will be allocated by the Program Chair to a suitable Regular, Work-in-Progress, or Poster tracks
  • All submissions in the IEEE CS proceedings format
PROCEEDINGS

  • All accepted papers will be published by IEEE (IEEE-DL and EI indexed) in Conference Proceedings.
  • Best Paper Awards will be granted to high quality papers.
  • Selected papers will be recommended to prestigious journal special issues.
