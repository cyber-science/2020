Invitation Letter and Visa Application Documents Request Instructions

If you are attending from outside of Canada, you will need to determine whether you require a Business Visitor Visa or Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) to travel to Canada. The following Government of Canada site helps you find out which requirement applies to you: http://www.cic.gc.ca/english/visit/visas.asp. Please closely read and follow the instructions for travel to Canada based on the country you are visiting from.

Next, follow the steps to apply for the travel documentation you require:

  1. ETA - https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/eta.html
  2. Business Visitor Visa - https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/business.html. It is your responsibility to ensure you understand and follow through with all of the requirements for a Business Visitor Visa to Canada.
    1. If you are travelling on a Business Visitor Visa, you will need us to provide a Letter of Invitation. Please complete the Letter of Invitation Template and email to group-docieee2020@athabascau.ca. If you require paper copies to be mailed to you, ensure that you include your full mailing address in the email.
    2. More information regarding the Letter of Invitation can be viewed here - https://www.canada.ca/en/immigration-refugees-citizenship/services/visit-canada/business.html.
    3. Ensure that you are applying early for your Business Visitor Visa to allow enough time to receive the Letter of Invitation and complete the Business Visitor Visa Process
    4. If you are attending as a participant (and not submitting a paper), you must complete the conference registration before the Letter of Invitation will be issued.
