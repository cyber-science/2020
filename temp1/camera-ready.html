Camera-Ready Submission

Important: The camera-ready submission will be made through IEEE CPS (Conference Publishing Services), NOT EDAS!


Please read the following notice before uploading your paper (for CyberSciTech, PICom, DASC, CBDCom, and all associated workshops/special sessions).

  1. The page limits are: Regular Papers: 8 + 2 extra pages for purchase, 6000 JPY per extra page
    WiP/Special Session/Workshop Papers: 6 + 2 extra pages for purchase, 6000 JPY per extra page
    Poster Papers: 4 + 2 extra pages for purchase, 6000 JPY per extra page

    Camera ready submissions must follow the following template format:
    https://www.computer.org/cms/CPS/app/8x11-2/instruct8.5x11x2.doc
    https://www.computer.org/cms/CPS/app/8x11-2/IEEECS_confs_LaTeX.zip

  2. The final upload deadline is 20 June 2019. The final camera-ready paper submission link at IEEE CPS will be available from 31st May, 2019.

  3. During preparation of your final camera-ready manuscript, any change to the list of authors and paper title must be informed with valid reasons by emailing cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com and must be approved by the conference chairs. Fail to do so, your paper may be delayed (or even excluded) from being included in the Proceedings.

  4. If you have any questions, please e-mail them to cyberscitechcongress@gmail.com, with the full author names and paper ID (the 10 digital ID number given by EDAS).
