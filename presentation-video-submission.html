Presentation Video Submission

How to make demo/presentation videos:
  • Authors are recommeded to download free source FlashBack Express to make demo videos.
  • All videos should be converted in .mp4 format;
  • The length of the video for a presentation should not be longer than 15 minutes.
Upload videos on cloud storage and send it to Web chair
  • Authors should upload videos on one of the cloud storages (e.g., Google Drive , OneDrive or Baidu NetDisk) and make it to a downloadable link.
  • Provide your video link to Web Chair Mr. Cheng-Li (Eric) Chen via email at cchen1@athabasca.edu.
