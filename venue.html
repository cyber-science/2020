Conference Venue and Local Information

The IEEE 2020 CyberSciTech/DASC/PICom/CBDCom Co-located Conferences are hosted by Athabasca University and the Faculty of Science and Technology. The conference location is in the Canadian city of Calgary. Calgary is located in the western province of Alberta and is about 125 kilometres east of the Banff National Park and 250 kilometres north of the Canada-US border. It is the 3rd largest city in Canada, with a population of nearly 1.3 million. One of Calgary's best known attractions is the annual Calgary Stampede, which bills itself as the "Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth" and is happening from July 3rd to 12th, 2020.

The conference venue and recommended hotel is the Calgary Marriot Downtown Hotel. There will be more information posted about the conference rates for this hotel once the conference registration is available.

Map and Directions

If you choose not to stay at the Mariott while you attend the conferences, you can also check out alternate places to stay. This venue is situated conveniently downtown and is located close to the C-Train and many bus routes. You can plan your transit ride through the Calgary Transit Website.

If you are staying at a different hotel and planning to drive to the Marriott, please be aware of the paid public parking nearby, which may have limited availability.
