Conference Venue and Local Information

The IEEE 2020 CyberSciTech/DASC/PICom/CBDCom Co-located Conferences are hosted by Athabasca University and the Faculty of Science and Technology. The conference location is Calgary, Alberta, Canada. Calgary is located in the western province of Alberta and is about 125 km. east of Banff National Park, and 250 km. north of the Canada-US border. It is the 3rd largest city in Canada, with a population of nearly 1.3 million. One of Calgary's most well-known attractions is the annual Calgary Stampede, which bills itself as the "Greatest Outdoor Show on Earth" and is happening on July 3-12, 2020.
